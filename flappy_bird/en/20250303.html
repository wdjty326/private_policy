Privacy Policy

2025-03-03 2025-01-01

This Privacy Policy explains how the Flappy Duck app collects and uses personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Our app collects the following information:

  • Through Google AdMob for advertising services:
    • Advertising ID (ADID)
    • IP address for ad serving
    • Ad click and impression information
  • Through Google Play Games and OAuth2:
    • Email address
    • Profile information (name, profile picture)
    • Game activity and achievements
    • Game progress and scores

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Providing personalized advertisements
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Preventing advertising fraud
  • User account creation and authentication
  • Saving and synchronizing game progress
  • Providing multiplayer functionality
  • Managing achievements and leaderboards

3. Information Sharing

Information collected through our app may be shared with Google LLC for Google AdMob services and Google Play Games services. Some of the collected information may be stored on Google's servers. For more information about Google's privacy practices, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

User information is retained for the duration of service use and will be deleted immediately upon account deletion request. Advertising identifier information will be deleted when its purpose is fulfilled.

5. User Rights

Users have the following rights:

  • Access, correction, and deletion of personal information
  • Reset of advertising identifier
  • Deactivation of personalized advertisements
  • Withdrawal of consent
  • Submission of inquiries regarding personal information

6. Google Play Games Integration

Our app integrates with Google Play Games services to provide features such as:

  • Game achievements and leaderboards
  • Cloud save functionality to sync game progress across devices
  • Social features including friend invites and multiplayer functionality

When you sign in with your Google account, we request the following permissions:

  • Email: To identify your account
  • Profile: To display your name and profile picture
  • Games: To access Google Play Games services

You can revoke these permissions at any time by signing out of Google Play Games in the app settings or by visiting your Google Account settings at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.

7. Security

This application implements appropriate security measures to protect users' personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we change this Privacy Policy, we will notify you through the app at least 7 days before the changes take effect.
